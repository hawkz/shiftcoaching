Tania Bright

Executive & Performance Coach

Passion Areas & Coaching Themes

Writers, public speaker training, senior executives, confidence, emotional intelligence, storytelling, entrepreneurs, female leaders, third sector and social enterprise, coaching with faith.

Past and current clients

AIB, Citibank, ASOS, The Salvation Army, Solace Women’s Aid and Action for Children.

Personal life

Tania is a veteran public speaker and published author, and as a result brings both empathy and deep practical insight to her clients, with an emphasis on resilience and self-development. Tania is a seasoned executive coach working with senior leaders in high impact corporate and charitable organisations. Having first worked herself in executive roles within the FTSE100 sector, she transitioned into senior leadership within the third sector focusing on critical humanitarian causes.

Tania brings a wealth of experience and highly personable approach to Shift Clients. Tania engages with clients goals using a variety of coaching techniques, undergirded by a firmly holistic approach, recognising that we are all multi-layered, body (physical well-being), mind (will and emotions) and spirit (creativity and soul).

Tania has had much joy in assisting others to achieve their “best selves” through this holistic approach – enabling the extraordinary to emerge; supporting entrepreneurs who’ve become stuck to break into their next stage of success; and business leaders to unlock vision, improving both group dynamic and bottom line.
