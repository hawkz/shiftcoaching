Life is full of different types of moments, every so often we have those big defining moments when the stakes are high, and the pressure’s on.

This is the right plan to get you on your A-game and ready to Carpe Diem both those planned and unexpected vital moments.

Maybe it’s an elevator pitch to potential investors or stakeholders, an interview, audition or casting moment; a staff presentation, conference or keynote; the prep for a big show, a sharing, performance or a video shoot. Whether it’s a regular occurrence or a one-off occasion, your Shift Coach will work with you to ensure that you have the focus, clarity and confidence to own the moment and make it count.

We’ll work with you to develop a workable schedule ensuring you are well prepared. We’ll unearth clarity on what it is that you are communicating, what it means to be authentic to your style, personality and values. We’ll explore how you come across and how to best engage your audience or prospect. We’ll share techniques on how to gain rapport, manage nerves and arrive with a winning mental attitude, so that you can leave the moment with a high five feeling knowing you gave your absolute best.
